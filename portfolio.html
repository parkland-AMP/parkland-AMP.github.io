About Us

Applied Media Promotions

AMP is a student-staffed strategic communications firm operated by the Fine and Applied Arts Department at Parkland College. Our students enhance their writing, critical thinking, and creative skills by producing promotional materials for nonprofits in the Champaign-Urbana community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve nonprofit clients and give Parkland students valuable work experience.

Our History

AMP Began in the summer of 2014 with three students and two projects-phase 1 of AMP's self-branding and the Parkland Entrepreneurial Network's branding and communication plan. We did not have a designated office. Students worked out of D023, a prop room, and D021, the photography classroom. The computers in the library were used when we were short on computers. The year was successful and we grew quickly and ended the 2014-15 school year on a high note, winning the Innovation Celebration Award for “Innovation in Engagement - Parkland College” in May of 2014. We were also highlighted at the Parkland Entrepreneur of the Year Banquet in June of 2014.
