Join Us

You've heard of hybrid cars, but have you heard of hybrid employees? Today's employers want workers with a variety of skills who are comfortable working on diverse teams.

That's exactly what we do at AMP.

We throw a bunch of different majors in a room and say 'Go!' (while advisors give input, and the occasional dessert item). Communication majors learn a little design, web developers learn a little video-editing, designers learn a little code (you get the picture), and we all learn how to keep clients happy while creating branding campaigns, websites, video, audio content, and much more.

We welcome students from all departments to apply for an internship. If you're ready to learn, we want you on our team! Apply today!
