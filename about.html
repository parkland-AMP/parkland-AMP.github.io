About AMP

Applied Media Promotions (AMP) is a student-staffed strategic communications firm operated by the Fine and Applied Arts Department at Parkland College. We provide students with the opportunity to enhance their writing, critical thinking, creative, and technical skills by producing promotional materials for organizations at Parkland and nonprofits in the Champaign-Urbana community.


We welcome students from all majors and ability levels.

Join our team!


What We do

AMP provides clients with strategic solutions to promotional challenges related to:



  • Branding and rebranding
  • Event planning and promotions
  • Advertisement creation
  • Media relation
  • Website design and development
  • Record Label & Music Production
